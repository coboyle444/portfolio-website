About Me

My name is Courtlyn O'Boyle and I grew up in the small hamlet of Sherwood Park, Alberta. I was born on February 1st, 2000 and remained in Sherwood Park up until July of 2024. I was raised alongside two older brothers which makes me rather competitive, though I would never admit that.

My hobbies as a child included dancing, singing, and creating videos. As an adult, I have grown to enjoy Twitch streaming, gaming, and anything that allows me to exercise the creative part of my brain. Living in a small town has given me a hunger for exploring more of what the world has to offer.
