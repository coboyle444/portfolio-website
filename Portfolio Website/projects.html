Double Exposure

I created this image in 2017 in photography class under the format of double exposure. Double exposure involves layering two images into one, typically with a silhouetted effect. The stairway in the background was from my high school, evoking a sense of feeling trapped in an institution like school. (I would like to assure that I do, actually, love school.)
